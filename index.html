Yoga Teacher Training Eligibility

To join the Yoga Teacher Training course at Rishikesh Yogpeeth, applicants must be 18 years and over, have a basic understanding of the English language (both written and spoken), and be physically and mentally prepared for the intensive course. In case of any physical challenges like bone or muscle issues, surgeries etc., speak to our senior consultant who can be contacted via WhatsApp provided on the website or drop us an email at info@rishikeshyogpeeth.com

  • For the 200-Hour and 500-Hour Yoga Teacher Training, no prior experience is required. It is designed for beginners to advanced practitioners. Kindly note that the 500 hrs course is a combination of 200 hrs followed by 300 hrs conducted in the same sequence.
  • Eligibility for the 500-Hour Yoga Teacher Training requires successful completion of a 200-Hour Yoga Teacher Training from a Yoga Alliance registered school.

Yoga Teacher Training Syllabus

Our Yoga Teacher Training syllabus is designed to meet the standards of Yoga Alliance. It offers one of the most comprehensive curriculums available, covering all the essential knowledge and skills required to become a certified yoga teacher. Here's what you'll learn:

Study of Patanjali Yoga Sutras, Bhagavad Gita, yogic lifestyle, ethics, self-discipline, and spiritual development.

Understanding anatomy, body systems, biomechanics, and the psychological effects of yoga.

Detailed alignment, benefits, contraindications, and sequencing of yoga postures.

Yogic breathing techniques for energy balance, emotional stability, and mental clarity.

Learning sacred mantras, pronunciation, chanting, and vibrational healing.

Mindfulness, awareness, concentration, and traditional meditation practices.

Class sequencing, cueing, adjustments, communication skills, and professional ethics.
